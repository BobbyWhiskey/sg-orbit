## Usage

### Default

A default tooltip.

<Preview>
    <Story name="default">
        <Tooltip
            content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
            trigger={<IconButton shape="circular"><AddIcon /></IconButton>}
        />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Position

A tooltip can be anchored at different positions.

The available positions are: `"top center"` `"top left"` `"top right"` `"bottom center"` `"bottom left"` `"bottom right"` `"right center"` `"left center"`.

<Preview>
    <Story name="position">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip position="top center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">TC</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="top left" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">TL</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="top right" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">TR</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">BC</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom left" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">BL</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom right" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">BR</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="right center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">RC</Button>} />
            <Tooltip position="left center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" shape="circular" size="lg">LC</Button>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

    <Story name="size">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip size="2xs" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary">Mini</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="xs" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary">Tiny</Button>} />

            <Tooltip content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary" >Medium</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="lg" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary">Large</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="2xl" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Button color="secondary">Huge</Button>} />

</Preview>

### Wide

A tooltip can be `wide` or `very wide` to allow for longer content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="wide">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip
                wide
                content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
                trigger={<Button color="secondary">Wide</Button>}
            />
            <Tooltip
                wide="very"
                content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
                trigger={<Button color="secondary">Very Wide</Button>}
            />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Flush

A tooltip can offload padding to its child.

<Preview>
    <Story name="flush">
        <Tooltip flush trigger={<IconButton shape="circular"><AddIcon /></IconButton>}>
            <div className="pa10">
                The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly.
            </div>
        </Tooltip>
    </Story>
</Preview>
