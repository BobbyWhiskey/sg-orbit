## Usage

### Default

A default tooltip.

<Preview>
    <Story name="default">
        <Tooltip
            content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
            trigger={<Button circular icon={<AddIcon />} />}
        />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Position

A tooltip can be anchored at different positions.

The available positions are: `"top center"` `"top left"` `"top right"` `"bottom center"` `"bottom left"` `"bottom right"` `"right center"` `"left center"`.

<Preview>
    <Story name="position">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip position="top center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">TC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="top left" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">TL</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="top right" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">TR</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">BC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom left" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">BL</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom right" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">BR</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="right center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">RC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="left center" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label circular size="large">LC</Label>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Size

A tooltip can vary in size.

Can be set to: `"mini"`, `"tiny"`, `"small"`, `"large"`, `"huge"` or omitted.

<Preview>
    <Story name="size">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip size="mini" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Mini</Label>} />
            <Tooltip size="tiny" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Tiny</Label>} />
            <Tooltip size="small" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Small</Label>} />
            <Tooltip content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Medium</Label>} />
            <Tooltip size="large" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Large</Label>} />
            <Tooltip size="huge" content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly." trigger={<Label>Huge</Label>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Wide

A tooltip can be `wide` or `very wide` to allow for longer content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="wide">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip
                wide
                content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
                trigger={<Label>Wide</Label>}
            />
            <Tooltip
                wide="very"
                content="The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly."
                trigger={<Label>Very Wide</Label>}
            />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Flush

A tooltip can offload padding to its child.

<Preview>
    <Story name="flush">
        <Tooltip flush trigger={<Button circular icon={<AddIcon />} />}>
            <div className="pa10">
                The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly.
            </div>
        </Tooltip>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Basic
