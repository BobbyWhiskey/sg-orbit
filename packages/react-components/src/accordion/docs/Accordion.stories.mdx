# Accordion

Accordions are commonly used to reduce the need to scroll when presenting multiple sections of content on a single page.

<ComponentInfo
    usage={"import { Accordion, Item, Header, Content, Text } from \"@orbit-ui/react-components\""}
    ariaPath="#accordion"
    githubPath="/packages/react-components/src/accordion/src"
/>

## Accessibility

The accordion header that shows and hides the content must be an [heading element](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/Heading_Elements).

That heading element should have a level that is appropriate for the information architecture of the page. You can do this by providing an `as` prop to the `<Header>` component.

## Usage

### Icon

An accordion item can contain [icons](?path=/docs/icon-gallery--page).

<Canvas>
    <Story name="icon">
        <Accordion>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">
                    <NotificationIcon />
                    <Text>Mars</Text>
                </Header>
                <Content>Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System.</Content>
            </Item>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">
                    <InfoIcon />
                    <Text>Jupiter</Text>
                </Header>
                <Content>Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.</Content>
            </Item>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">
                    <HelpIcon />
                    <Text>Venus</Text>
                </Header>
                <Content>Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.</Content>
            </Item>
        </Accordion>
    </Story>
</Canvas>

### Multiple

An accordion can allow multiple items to be expanded at once.

<Canvas>
    <Story name="multiple">
        <Accordion multiple>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">Mars</Header>
                <Content>Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System.</Content>
            </Item>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">Jupiter</Header>
                <Content>Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.</Content>
            </Item>
            <Item>
                <Header as="h3">Venus</Header>
                <Content>Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.</Content>
            </Item>
        </Accordion>
    </Story>
</Canvas>

### Render props

An accordion item can update is content based on the `isOpen` value by providing a render prop function.

<Canvas>
    <Story name="render props">
        <Accordion>
            <Item>
                {({ isOpen }) => (
                    <>
                        <Header className={isOpen ? "primary-600" : undefined} as="h3">
                            Mars
                        </Header>
                        <Content>Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet.</Content>
                    </>
                )}
            </Item>
            <Item>
                {({ isOpen }) => (
                    <>
                        <Header className={isOpen ? "primary-600" : undefined} as="h3">
                            Jupiter
                        </Header>
                        <Content>Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.</Content>
                    </>
                )}
            </Item>
            <Item>
                {({ isOpen }) => (
                    <>
                        <Header className={isOpen ? "primary-600" : undefined} as="h3">
                            Venus
                        </Header>
                        <Content>Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.</Content>
                    </>
                )}
            </Item>
        </Accordion>
    </Story>
</Canvas>

### Data items

You can supply accordion items with an array instead of using the granular components.

<Canvas>
    <Story name="data render">
        <Accordion aria-label="Planets">
            {[
                { header: "Mars", content: "Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet." },
                { header: "Jupiter", content: "Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System." },
                { header: "Venus", content: "Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty." }
            ]
                .map(({ header, content }) =>
                    <Item key={header}>
                        <Header as="h3">{header}</Header>
                        <Content>{content}</Content>
                    </Item>
                )}
        </Accordion>
    </Story>
</Canvas>
