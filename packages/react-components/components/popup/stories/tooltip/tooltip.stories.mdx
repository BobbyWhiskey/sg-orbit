## Usage

### Default

A default tooltip.

<Preview>
    <Story name="default">
        <Tooltip content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Position

A tooltip can be anchored at different positions.

Can be set to: `"top center"` `"top left"` `"top right"` `"bottom center"` `"bottom left"` `"bottom right"` `"right center"` `"left center"` or omitted.

<Preview>
    <Story name="position">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip position="top center" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">TC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="top left" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">TL</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="top right" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">TR</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom center" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">BC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom left" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">BL</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="bottom right" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">BR</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="right center" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">RC</Label>} />
            <Tooltip position="left center" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Label circular size="large">LC</Label>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Size

A tooltip can vary in size.

Can be set to: `"mini"`, `"tiny"`, `"small"`, `"large"`, `"huge"` or omitted.

<Preview>
    <Story name="size">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip size="mini" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Mini</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="tiny" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Tiny</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="small" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Small</Button>} />
            <Tooltip content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Medium</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="large" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Large</Button>} />
            <Tooltip size="huge" content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Huge</Button>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Wide

A tooltip can be `wide` or `very wide` to allow for longer content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="wide">
        <div className="flex items-space-between">
            <Tooltip wide content="This is a wide tooltip which allows for lots of content with additional space. You can fit a lot of words here and the paragraphs will be pretty wide." trigger={<Button>Wide</Button>} />
            <Tooltip wide="very" content="This is a wide tooltip which allows for lots of content with additional space. You can fit a lot of words here and the paragraphs will be pretty wide." trigger={<Button>Very Wide</Button>} />
        </div>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Flush

A tooltip can offload padding to its child.

<Preview>
    <Story name="flush">
        <Tooltip flush trigger={<Button circular secondary icon={<AddIcon />} />}>
            <div className="pa10">
                Adds users to your feed
            </div>
        </Tooltip>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Basic

A tootip can provide more basic formatting.

<Preview>
    <Story name="basic">
        <Tooltip basic content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Inverted

A tooltip can have its colours inverted.

<Preview>
    <Story name="inverted">
        <Tooltip inverted content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

A disabled tooltip only renders its trigger.

<Preview>
    <Story name="disabled">
        <Tooltip disabled content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
