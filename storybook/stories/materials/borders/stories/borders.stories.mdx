## Colors

Tachyons original borders colors have been replaced with [Orbit foundation colors](?path=/docs/materials-colors--page).

For every colors of Orbit foundation the following classes are available:

- b--[_color-name_]-[_50-900_] (ex. `b--sunary-50`)

- hover-b--[_color-name_]-[_50-900_] (ex. `hover-b--sunary-50`)
