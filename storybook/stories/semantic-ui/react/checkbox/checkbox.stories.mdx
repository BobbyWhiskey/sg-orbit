## Usage

### Labelled

A checkbox can have a label.

<Preview>
    <Story name="labelled">
        <Checkbox label="Milky Way" />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Checked

A checkbox can be checked.

<Preview>
    <Story name="checked">
        <Checkbox defaultChecked label="Milky Way" />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Indeterminate

A checkbox can be indeterminate.

<Preview>
    <Story name="indeterminate">
        <Checkbox defaultIndeterminate label="Supernova" />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Disabled

A checkbox can be disabled.

<Preview>
    <Story name="disabled">
        <Checkbox disabled label="Milky Way" />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Complex label

A checkbox can have a complex label.

<Preview>
    <Story name="complex label">
        <Checkbox label={<label>Outlook Email <CommunicationIcon className="ml1" /></label>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### No label

A checkbox doesn't require a label.

<Preview>
    <Story name="no label">
        <Checkbox />
    </Story>
</Preview>
