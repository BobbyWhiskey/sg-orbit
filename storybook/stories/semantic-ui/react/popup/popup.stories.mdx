# Popup

## Installation

<Installation imports={<Imports />} />

## Usage

### Default

A default popup.

<Preview>
    <Story name="default">
        <Popup content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Wide

A popup can be wide to allow for longer content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="wide">
        <Popup wide content="This is a wide pop-up which allows for lots of content with additional space. You can fit a lot of words here and the paragraphs will be pretty wide." trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Very Wide

A popup can be very wide to allow for longer content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="very wide">
        <Popup wide="very" content="This is a wide pop-up which allows for lots of content with additional space. You can fit a lot of words here and the paragraphs will be pretty wide." trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Flush

A popup can offload padding to its child.

<Preview>
    <Story name="flush">
        <Popup trigger={<Button>Read</Button>} className="flush">
            <Popup.Content className="pa10">
                <div>Hello is it tea you are looking for?</div>
            </Popup.Content>
        </Popup>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Basic

A popup can provide more basic formatting.

<Preview>
    <Story name="basic">
        <Popup basic content="Adds users to your feed" trigger={<Button>Add</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Inverted

A popup can have its colours inverted.

<Preview>
    <Story name="inverted">
        <Popup inverted content="Once upon a midnight dreary, while I pondered, weak and weary, Over many a quaint and curious volume of forgotten lore—" trigger={<Button>Read</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Disabled

A disabled popup only renders its trigger.

<Preview>
    <Story name="disabled">
        <Popup disabled content="Once upon a midnight dreary, while I pondered, weak and weary, Over many a quaint and curious volume of forgotten lore—" trigger={<Button>Read</Button>} />
    </Story>
</Preview>
